\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
IDMT Protection Curve Implementations. 

Implements IEC 60255-\/3 and IEEE C37.\+112 time-\/current characteristics (TCC).

IEC Formula:\+ T = TMS $\ast$ k /\+ (M$^\wedge$α -\/ 1) IEEE Formula:\+ T = TDS $\ast$ [A/\+(M$^\wedge$p -\/ 1) + B]

Where M = I /\+ I\+\_\+pickup (current multiplier)

Three-\/step process:\+
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Measure DC offset (average value -\/ signals are biased to midpoint)
